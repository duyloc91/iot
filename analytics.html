Recommendation of Operational Windows

With the risk of power shortage as a constant thread, the team aims to use the historical consumption trends throughout the previous days to recommend the operational window for the components, in order to maximize their utilities to the public.

Reductions on operations will help preserve power for more important components such as the Compactor and the Board.
